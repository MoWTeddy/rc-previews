Reading notes. The consistent pairings are A+D (all mono: sharp, modern, matches the current nav) or B+C (all serif: warmer, editorial, matches the site's text). The current site mixes A+C — which is what you spotted. The serif wordmark (B) is handsome but reads softer in the nav next to mono links; if B wins, the nav links should probably go serif small-caps too. The mono R (D) is the more distinctive compact mark — serif Rs are common in heritage branding. Whichever pair wins, the drawn gold asterisk stays identical in all of them.
