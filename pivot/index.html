Testimonials

The business that can't run without you is the one that owns you.

Every hour of the week is spoken for, and the business still asks for more. Most owners answer by working harder - and build a company that cannot run without them. It costs them twice: their time now, and their value later. Buyers pay a premium for companies that run themselves, and discount the rest - if they buy them at all.

The usual answers are another hire, another framework, another consultant. But what actually changes both numbers - the hours and the valuation - is the judgement of owners who have already done it: built a business that ran without them, then sold it well.

That judgement is almost impossible to buy. So we put it in a room, on your side of the table.
