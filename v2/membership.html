Membership

What you get for £1,000 a month.

Part one

What membership includes.

One fee, one goal: a more valuable company and a better exit, sooner. Five ways we get you there.

No. 1

Rare Advisors

The expertise of a full advisory team, without hiring one.

Throughout your membership

Most owners lean on a single adviser - one perspective, one network, one set of incentives. Rare Company gives you a whole bench of proven specialists: valuation, growth, finance, tax, legal, leadership, M&A and exit.

Bring us the issue; we bring the right people to solve it. Preparing for a sale, weighing an offer, restructuring, taking yourself out of the day-to-day, or deciding what will lift value fastest - you get practical advice from people who know what buyers pay for and what makes a company worth more.

No inflated consulting projects. No adviser pushing their own product. Just the right expertise, at the moment it matters.

No. 2

Rare Peers

A private group of owners at your stage - and a call away when a big decision lands.

Monthly · plus whenever it can't wait

You sit with a small group of owners at a similar stage, scale and level of ambition. The same people meet every month, so they know your company, your goals and the decisions in front of you.

This isn't networking. It's a standing group of experienced peers who challenge your thinking, share what worked for them, and tell you what the people around you won't.

Between meetings, we're there when a decision can't wait. A buyer approaches. A senior hire goes wrong. An adviser pushes a course of action. Send us the question and we connect you with members who've already faced it - you still make the call, just with better information and fewer blind spots.

No. 3

Rare Experiences

Dinners and trips where the real deal-talk happens.

Regularly

Private dinners, breakfasts, sporting occasions, cultural visits and overseas trips - brought together with serious owners, exited founders and trusted leaders. No badges, no speeches, no rooms full of people selling to each other.

Every experience is composed around a small number of relevant people: owners at similar stages, founders who've already banked meaningful exits, people with genuine context and no agenda.

The result is better conversation and the kind of insight that rarely goes public - what buyers are really paying, how deals are structured, which advisers deliver, and what selling actually feels like from the inside.

No. 4

Rare Directory

The advisers and specialists worth hiring - rated by owners who've used them.

Always open · Vetted · Member-rated

Finding the right corporate-finance adviser, lawyer, tax specialist, wealth manager or senior operator can take years of trial, error and expensive mistakes. The Directory is the shortcut.

Every name is recommended by members, vetted by us, and reviewed by owners who've hired them for real - what they're good at, what they charged, how they held up under pressure, and whether members would appoint them again.

No paid listings. No sponsorship. No advisers using the membership as a sales pipeline. A trusted network that would normally take a career to build.

No. 5

Your Rare Company

Your plan to a more valuable company - and the right exit, sooner.

From day one · Reviewed throughout

Membership begins with a private assessment of your company: what it's worth today and how ready it is for a future sale. We identify what's lifting value, what's holding it down, and the few priorities that will make the biggest difference - then help you turn them into progress.

Your valuation doesn't get filed and forgotten. It becomes a working number: revisited as the company changes, tested against real buyer appetite, and formally reviewed at least twice a year.

You'll always know what your company might be worth today, what could make it worth significantly more, and what needs to happen between now and the exit you want. Around that plan sit the advisers, peers, introductions and accountability to move it forward - a stronger company, a higher valuation, and a faster, better-prepared route to sale.

House rules

How the membership is run.

  1. i.In confidence

    What is said in the room stays in the room. No notes, no quotes, no exceptions.

  2. ii.Experience, not theory

    Every table is anchored by people who have done the thing, not studied it, advised on it or posted about it.

  3. iii.No pitching

    Nothing is sold in the room. Not services, not deals, not yourself. Nobody here wants your money.

  4. iv.By name

    There are no open tickets. Every seat is considered, and every member is there by name.

  5. v.Rare settings

    Private dining rooms, townhouses and tables you cannot book. The setting is part of the conversation.

Part three

Who it's for.

Owners and leaders of established UK businesses who plan to sell for multi-millions - and know that the right support, from the right people, is what makes that happen. Plus a few exited founders, who sit on your side of the table for the company, not for a fee.

We keep the membership deliberately small, and we don't admit advisers, brokers, or anyone who would treat the room as a pipeline. Nothing is sold inside Rare Company, and nobody in the room wants your money.
