Testimonials

Selling is a game the other side plays every day.

Buyers compete for prepared companies - and pick off the rest. The most valuable thing you'll ever sell is the one thing you've never sold; the buyer will have done it many times, their advisers hundreds. That asymmetry - not effort, not luck - is what decides most deals.

The usual answer is advisory: capable, expensive, and engaged at the end, once the decision is made and most of the outcome is already set. Advisers matter - at the right moment. But what actually changes the number starts years earlier: the shape of the business, the timing, and the judgement of owners who have already been through it.

That judgement is the one thing you cannot buy at the point of sale. So we put it in a room much earlier, on your side of the table.
