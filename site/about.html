About us

About us.

In our words

A letter from the founders.

We built Rare Company because when we needed it for our own multi-million-pound exits, it didn't exist.

We each spent years building businesses we intended, one day, to sell. In all that time, the conversations that would have mattered most - what it's actually worth, when to go, who to trust, what a deal feels like from the inside - happened rarely, late, or not at all. Owners don't have colleagues. And by the time the advisers arrive, most of the outcome is already set.

So after our own exits, we built the room we had gone looking for: small, candid, confidential, and filled with people who have either done the thing or are seriously setting about it.

Our promise is simple. We will keep the room small and the bar high. We will never let anyone sell to you inside it. And we will tell you the truth - about your business, your timing, and whether we can genuinely help - even when it costs us a member.

Eddie Whittingham Founder
Astan Morarji Founder
