Testimonials

Selling is a game the other side plays every day.

The most valuable thing you'll ever sell is the one thing you've never sold. The buyer will have done it many times; their advisers, hundreds. That asymmetry - not effort, not luck - is what decides most deals.

The usual answer is advisory: capable, expensive, and engaged at the end, once the decision is made and most of the outcome is already set. Advisers matter - at the right moment. But what actually changes the number starts years earlier: the shape of the business, the timing, and the judgement of owners who have already been through it.

That judgement is the one thing you cannot buy at the point of sale. So we put it in a room much earlier, on your side of the table.

The fit

Who it's for.

Owners and co-founders of established UK businesses - with a realistic exit value of £2 million or more - who intend to sell, in whole or in part, within the next five years. And a small number of exited founders, who sit on your side of the table for the company, not for a fee.

We keep the membership deliberately small, and we don't admit advisers, brokers, or anyone who would treat the room as a pipeline. Nothing is sold inside Rare Company, and nobody in the room wants your money. That is what makes the conversations worth having.
